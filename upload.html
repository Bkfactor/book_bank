Academic Directors

Submit a Study Material

Contribute course notes, past questions, textbooks or slides for your department.

How it works

1

Upload file to Google Drive

Upload your material to Google Drive and set sharing to "Anyone with the link can view". Copy the shareable link.

2

Fill the submission form

Complete the form with the course code, title, faculty, department, level and semester. Paste your Drive link.

3

Goes live for students

After a quick review, the material is added to the Book Bank and becomes accessible to all students in that department.

File naming guide

Use this format for Drive file names:

[COURSE CODE] [TYPE] [SEMESTER] [YEAR]
e.g. CSC 302 Past Questions S1 2024
