Academic Directors & Course Reps

Submit a Study Material

Contribute course notes, past questions, textbooks or slides for your department. Every material submitted helps a fellow LCU student.

How it works

1

Upload to Google Drive

Upload your file to Google Drive and set sharing to "Anyone with the link can view". Copy the shareable link.

2

Fill the submission form

Complete the form with course code, title, faculty, department, level and semester. Paste your Drive link.

3

Goes live for students

After a quick review by the Academic Directorate, the material is added to the Book Bank and becomes accessible to all students.

File naming guide

Name your Drive files using this format:

[COURSE CODE] [TYPE] [SEMESTER] [YEAR]
e.g. CSC302 Past Questions S2 2024
